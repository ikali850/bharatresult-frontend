Educational Qualifications
Criteria Details

Physical and Age Requirements
Criteria Details
Age Limit 18-23 years as of 01-01-2025 (Age relaxation: SC/ST - 5 years, OBC - 3 years, Ex-Servicemen - 3 years after deduction of military service)
Height (Male) 170 cms (Relaxation: ST - 162.5 cms, Other categories - 165.0 cms)
Height (Female) 157 cms (Relaxation: ST - 150.0 cms, Other categories - 155.0 cms)
Chest (Male) Unexpanded - 80 cms, Minimum Expansion - 5 cms (Relaxation: ST - 76 cms, Other categories - 78 cms)

Physical Efficiency Test (Female) 1.6 Kms in 8 1/2 minutes (Ladakh Region: 800 meters in 5 minutes)
Medical Standards Good mental and bodily health, free from any physical defect likely to interfere with the efficient performance of duties
Nationality Indian
Domicile Submit domicile certificate of the State/UT indicated in the online application form at the time of Document Verification

How to Apply

  • One-Time Registration: Visit the official SSC website, click on ‘Register Now’ and complete the registration process.
  • Filling the Online Application Form: Log in using your Registration Number and Password, fill in the required details, and upload your photograph and signature.
  • Photograph and Signature: Ensure your photograph and signature meet the specified requirements before uploading.
  • Preview and Verify: Double-check all entered details and ensure everything is correct before final submission.
  • Submit: Submit the application form online.
  • Correction Window: If needed, use the correction window period to make any necessary changes to your application form.
